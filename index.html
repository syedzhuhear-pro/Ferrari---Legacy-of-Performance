ABOUT FERRARI

Driven by Passion, Powered by Innovation

Ferrari has become one of the world's most recognized automotive brands, admired for its commitment to performance, elegant design, and motorsport excellence. Every Ferrari reflects decades of engineering expertise, innovation, and Italian craftsmanship, creating an unforgettable driving experience.

From legendary road cars to championship-winning Formula One machines, Ferrari continues to inspire enthusiasts with its relentless pursuit of speed, precision, and perfection.

OUR HISTORY

A Legacy Built on Speed

1939

The Beginning

Enzo Ferrari founded Auto Avio Costruzioni, marking the beginning of an extraordinary journey in motorsport and automotive excellence.

1947

First Ferrari

The Ferrari 125 S became the first vehicle to bear the Ferrari name, powered by a remarkable V12 engine.

1950

Formula One

Ferrari entered the inaugural Formula One World Championship and became the only team to compete in every season since.

Today

Global Icon

Ferrari continues to inspire the world through innovation, racing success, and legendary road cars.

ICONIC CARS

Legends That Defined Ferrari

Ferrari F40

Introduced in 1987, the F40 became one of Ferrari's most celebrated supercars, combining lightweight engineering with breathtaking performance.

LaFerrari

Ferrari's first hybrid hypercar, blending Formula One technology with extraordinary speed and cutting-edge design.

Ferrari Enzo

Named after Ferrari's founder, the Enzo represents advanced aerodynamics, Formula One inspiration, and unmatched prestige.

SCUDERIA FERRARI

Racing Through History

Ferrari is the only team to have competed in every Formula One World Championship since 1950. Decades of innovation, championship victories, and unforgettable drivers have made Scuderia Ferrari one of the greatest names in motorsport.
